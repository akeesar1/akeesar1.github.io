Instructions

Feeding Your Koala

Koalas mostly eat eucalyptus leaves. Make sure to offer fresh leaves daily.

Sleeping Habits

Koalas sleep 18 to 22 hours a day! Provide a comfy, safe tree branch or hammock.

Creating a Cozy Home

Set up a quiet, safe space for your koala with branches, bedding, and toys.

Daily Care

Spend time checking your koala’s health, cleaning their area, and offering gentle attention.
