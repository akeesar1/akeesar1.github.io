Website Evaluations

Well Designed Website

Apple

  • Contrast: It uses strong contrast with large bold headlines and slideshow for new products.
  • Repetition: Apple uses the same fonts and color scheme making the website look clean.
  • Alignment: Most of the content is alligned very neatly, the text and images line up making it very structured and professional.
  • Proximity: Product name, price and description are all grouped together with proper spacing, this makes it easy to understand the information.

Poorly Designed Website

Yahoo

  • Contrast: Low contrast between text and background on many pages makes text difficult to read.
  • Repetition: Inconsistent use of fonts, colors, and styles; links and headers do not follow a pattern, leading to visual confusion.
  • Alignment: Layout feels cluttered.
  • Proximity: Ads and content are too close together.
